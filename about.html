About Us

EventsInvest is Nigeria's first crowdfunding network where ideas meet opportunity.

INTRODUCTION

Now you can get your events crowdfunded by Sponsors donation or crowdfunding investment by offering a percentage interest to the public who crowdfund your event!

Share the cake of your success amongst friends, fans and well wishers. For individuals that are creatives in Entertainment, Arts, Educational, Craft, Business, technology, Religious, Virtual, or Social and Ceremonial events, you can now finance your event through our crowdfunding platform and reward your friends,fans and investors with a percentage interest from your generated revenue.
