Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Below are our frequently asked questions (FAQs)

You can join by signing up as an investor or an event organizer. Visit the homepage and click Sign up.

After you have signed up, you will need to fund your wallet in the user dashboard, Click wallet, input the amount you would like to fund and click Add funds. You will be redirected to our payment gateway. Add your card details and you are good to go!

As soon as you have funded your wallet and invested the funds in your event of choice, you will wait till a day after the date of the event, which is the start of a Pay Period or investment return date (IRD). This Pay period runs for 2 days or 48 working hours after the event date.This is when we start processing payments. Your initial investment capital and accrued interest will then be credited into your user wallet, then you can withdraw to your local bank. Please allow 24 to 48 hours for your wallets to be funded.

Time is money, so an investor that put his money on an investment for 30 days desevres more profit than an investor that only put his money for 15 days. First half investors earn more than second half investors.

So if you have 20 investors, you will be returning the initial capital of these 20 investors and an accrued interest for 21 investors with the median investment figure duplicated. This minimal fee will be added to your investment payback figure at funding. If you are an event organiser that bought our subscription plan, you are exempted from paying the Platform charges.

There are no fees charged for deposits. We charge 3% (processing fees) + #200 naira (Tax withholding) on all withdrawals. No additional hidden charges. This means to withdraw 10,000 naira we charge you #550 naira only. To withdraw Cryptocurrency we charge 3.5% of withdrawal value after the blockchain transaction charges applied by the blockchain network. Fair enough!

Full term is when you invest early in an event. Events take alot of organising and planning, if you invest early it helps the organiser to plan properly. Full term investors get full interest rate preset by the organisers, while half term investors get half interest rate of the initial rate preset by the organisers.

Investors pool is a lounge of investors. Each investors pool tells you how many investors are allowed to fund an event.

Investors pool for each event closes 48 hours before the event date.

Investment timeline is the duration of time that an organizer is allowed to raise funds from his/her investors pool. Early investors would get full interest rate as preset by the organizer. Late investors may get half interest rate because of a reduced time span of thier investment.
